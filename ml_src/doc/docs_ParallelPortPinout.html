Parallel Port Pinout

See this for installing the parallel port driver. Port0 is only for output (12 lines) and Port1, input (5 lines).

Pin Port0 Port1 ----- ----- ----- 1 p0.8 2 p0.0 3 p0.1 4 p0.2 5 p0.3 6 p0.4 7 p0.5 8 p0.6 9 p0.7 10 p1.3 11 p1.4 12 p1.2 13 p1.1 14 p0.9 15 p1.0 16 p0.10 17 p0.11 18-25 GND ----- ----- ----- Port0: OUT only Port1: IN only
